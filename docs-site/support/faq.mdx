<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need a subscription to use ClawBox?">
    No. The hardware is yours. You can run ClawBox with your own AI provider key
    (Claude, GPT, Gemini, OpenRouter) or local models. A ClawBox AI subscription is
    optional and adds managed AI plus higher support tiers. See
    [Subscriptions](/guides/subscriptions).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which AI models can it run?">
    ClawBox AI by default, plus Claude, GPT, Gemini, and OpenRouter as first-class
    options. Local Ollama / llama.cpp models are supported too. See
    [Choose Your AI Provider](/setup/choose-ai-provider).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What's the difference between Connect and Workstation?">
    **ClawBox Connect** (Jetson Orin Nano, €549) is a low-power always-on assistant.
    **ClawBox Workstation** (DGX Spark, €5,499) is a personal AI supercomputer that runs
    large models locally. Full comparison on the
    [Workstation page](/hardware/clawbox-workstation).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which chat apps does it support?">
    Telegram and the on-device web chat are the managed ClawBox paths today.
    Advanced users can add other OpenClaw channels manually when their installed
    OpenClaw version and plugins support them. See [Messaging Channels](/guides/messaging-channels).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How much power does it use?">
    ClawBox Connect draws ~7–15 W (about €39/year in electricity). ClawBox Workstation
    peaks around 300 W under heavy load.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where do you ship?">
    ClawBox ships worldwide via DHL Express from Bulgaria. Shipping cost and time are
    shown at checkout on [clawbox.tech](https://clawbox.tech).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I get support?">
    Email [yanko@idrobots.com](mailto:yanko@idrobots.com) or join the community Discord.
    Support depth depends on your subscription tier — see
    [Subscriptions](/guides/subscriptions).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
