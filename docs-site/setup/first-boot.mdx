Get your ClawBox up and running in about **5 minutes**. No tools required — just a
Wi-Fi or Ethernet connection.

<Info>
**Setup time:** ~5 minutes · **Tools:** none · **Needed:** Wi-Fi or Ethernet
</Info>

## What's in the box

- ClawBox unit (NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano 8 GB)
- 512 GB NVMe SSD with OpenClaw pre-installed
- 20 W USB-C power adapter
- Ethernet cable
- Quick-start card with QR code

## Setup steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Unbox your ClawBox">
    Remove it from the packaging, check all components are present, and place it on a
    flat, ventilated surface. Keep the quick-start card handy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to your network">
    Plug the Ethernet cable into your router (recommended), or use Wi-Fi — the setup
    wizard will guide you. A stable connection matters for first-time setup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Power on">
    Connect the power adapter and plug it into an outlet. Wait for the status LED to turn
    **solid green** (~60 seconds).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pair your device">
    Open your phone camera or a QR scanner and scan the QR code on the quick-start card.
    Connect Telegram when prompted, then follow the pairing wizard to link your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start chatting">
    Send your first message to test the connection. Try a question or a command, then
    explore features like browser automation and voice.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
A reply to your first message means your ClawBox is live. 🦀
</Check>

## Reaching the device directly

If you prefer the browser setup page, visit `http://clawbox.local` from a device on the
same network.

<Warning>
If `clawbox.local` doesn't load, **disable any active VPN** first (the most common
cause), or use the device's IP address from your router's client list:
`http://<device-ip>`. Some networks also block mDNS — Ethernet is the most reliable path.
</Warning>
