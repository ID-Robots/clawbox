# First Boot

The first time you power on your ClawBox, it runs a guided setup wizard in your browser.

## 1. Connect power

Plug the included USB-C power supply into the ClawBox. For the most reliable first
setup, also connect an **Ethernet cable** — you can switch to Wi-Fi during setup.

The device boots in well under a minute.

## 2. Reach the device

Open a browser on a phone or computer **on the same network** and go to:

```
http://clawbox.local
```

<Note>
If `clawbox.local` doesn't load (some networks block mDNS), find the device's IP
address from your router's client list, or connect over Ethernet and try again.
Then visit `http://<device-ip>`.
</Note>

## 3. Follow the wizard

The on-device wizard walks you through:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Network">
    Confirm Ethernet or join your Wi-Fi network.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI provider">
    Keep ClawBox AI, or connect your own provider key.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Channels">
    Link the chat apps you want to message your assistant from.
  </Step>
</Steps>

When the wizard finishes, your ClawBox is ready to use.

## Resetting setup

If you need to start over, you can re-run the wizard from the device settings page,
or hold the reset procedure described in your unit's printed insert. When in doubt,
[contact support](mailto:yanko@idrobots.com).
