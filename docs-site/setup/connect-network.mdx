---
title: "Connect to a Network"
summary: "Get your ClawBox online over Ethernet or Wi-Fi."
---

ClawBox needs an internet connection to reach your AI provider and chat channels.

## Ethernet (recommended for setup)

Plug an Ethernet cable into the ClawBox before powering on. It connects automatically —
no configuration needed. This is the most reliable path for first-time setup.

## Wi-Fi

From the setup page at `http://clawbox.local`:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open network settings">
    Go to the **Network** section of the setup wizard or settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your network">
    Choose your Wi-Fi network from the list and enter the password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Wait for the connection to confirm. The device keeps your network saved for next boot.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Double-check the Wi-Fi password if the connection fails — a wrong password is the most
common cause. The setup page will tell you when authentication fails.
</Warning>

## Finding the device IP

If `clawbox.local` won't resolve on your network, locate the ClawBox in your router's
connected-devices list to get its IP address, then browse to `http://<device-ip>`.
