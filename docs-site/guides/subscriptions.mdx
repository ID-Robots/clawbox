# ClawBox AI Subscriptions

ClawBox hardware is yours to own. A **ClawBox AI subscription** is optional and powers
the default managed AI experience plus higher support tiers.

## What a subscription adds

- Managed **ClawBox AI** model access without bringing your own provider key.
- Higher **support tiers** for setup and configuration help.

<Note>
You can always run ClawBox with **your own** provider key (Claude, GPT, Gemini,
OpenRouter) or **local models** instead — see [Choose Your AI Provider](/setup/choose-ai-provider).
</Note>

## Tiers & support

| Tier | AI access | Support |
|---|---|---|
| No subscription | Bring your own key / local models | Hardware, warranty, shipping, billing + **basic setup guidance** by email |
| **Pro** | Managed ClawBox AI | **Email support** for setup & configuration |
| **Max** | Managed ClawBox AI | **Priority + video support** for setup & configuration |

## Managing your subscription

Subscriptions are billed through Stripe. Use the customer portal link in your billing
email to update payment details, change plan, or cancel.
