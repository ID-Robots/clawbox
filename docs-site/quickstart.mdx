Get from a sealed box to a working AI assistant in a few minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Power on">
    Connect the included USB-C power supply and (optionally) an Ethernet cable.
    The status light comes on and ClawBox boots in under a minute.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the setup page">
    From a phone or laptop on the same network, visit [http://clawbox.local](http://clawbox.local).
    If that doesn't resolve, use the device's IP address (see [First Boot](/setup/first-boot)).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to the internet">
    Join your Wi-Fi or confirm the Ethernet connection. See [Connect to a Network](/setup/connect-network).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick your AI provider">
    ClawBox AI works out of the box. To use Claude, GPT, Gemini, or a local model,
    follow [Choose Your AI Provider](/setup/choose-ai-provider).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link a chat app">
    Connect Telegram, WhatsApp, or Discord and message your ClawBox.
    See [Messaging Channels](/guides/messaging-channels).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
  Send a message like "Hello, what can you do?" from your linked chat app. A reply means your ClawBox is live.
</Check>
